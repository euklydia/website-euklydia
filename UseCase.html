01 the challenge

The client faced challenges with a previous agency, observing a daily decline in Facebook ROI and minimal engagement. They discovered Eyklydia through a satisfied client, seeking a dependable partner for their account.

02 The Strategy

This account was in dire need of improvement. Leveraging our in-house AI, we scrutinized engaging keywords, ideal posting times, and relevant topics. With our analysis, we predicted ad copy, campaign content, and strategy reactions, crafting a precise Facebook strategy tailored to the target audience. This included optimizing match types, segregating brand from non-brand keywords, and pausing underperforming ads. With a new foundation set, our team focused on increasing sales volume and optimizing efficiency.

Additionally, to enhance user experience, we provided a customized rebranding solution tailored to the specific needs of the target client.

03 The Result

In just one month, remarkable results have been achieved. Revenue surged by 1.8%, showcasing tangible growth. Our strategic overhaul on Facebook led to an impressive increase in reach by 128.9%, while Instagram followers soared by 120.5%. Additionally, Facebook followers surged by 65.7%. With an expansion to Instagram, restored trust, and a clearly defined brand persona, the client's business is poised for continued success.
