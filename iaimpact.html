Our Vision for a Greener AI

At Eyklydia, we utilize transfer learning and federated learning methods to handle large volumes of data in real-time, effectively reducing carbon emissions while promoting sustainable innovation instead of data center. Transfer learning leverages pre-trained models, minimizing the need for extensive computations and energy-intensive training processes. Federated learning decentralizes data processing, allowing for collaborative model training across multiple devices without centralizing data in energy-consuming data centers. This approach not only reduces energy consumption but also enhances privacy and security by keeping data localized. As a result, our innovative approach aligns with our commitment to eco-friendly practices while driving sustainable innovation in the energy transition.
