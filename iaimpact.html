The adoption of AI has not only grown significantly over the years but has also become increasingly environmentally conscious. In 2010, a mere 15% of companies were implementing sustainable AI practices. This number has grown dramatically as awareness and adoption of eco-friendly approaches have increased across the corporate landscape. By 2021, these efforts resulted in a substantial 40% reduction in CO2 emissions per computational unit, underscoring a strong commitment to more sustainable AI usage that continues to drive innovation and growth.
